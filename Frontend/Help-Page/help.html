How do I sign in to my account?
On the home page you'll see a button that says "Personalize your experience" that you can press. It will take you on the Register & Log In Page. Also, there is the possibility to press the "Account" button on the right corner from the top bar.
How do I add a book to my shelves?
On the page where you can view a certain book, you also have on the left side some options for adding that book to your shelf.
How do I remove a book from my Reading Challenge?
You can't really delete it only from the Reading Challenge, but you can delete it from your "Read" shelf and it will be extracted from the total number of books read during the challenge that you made.
How can I set or delete my Readding Challenge goal?
On the page "Challenges" you can see all of your goals, as well as the edit/delete a goal. At the bottom of the page, you also have a button "Add new goal!".
How can I discconnect from my account?
When pressing the button "Account" from the right part of the top bar, you'll be redirected to a page of general setting where there will be the log out option.
